About Me

Personal Details

Ever since I was a child, videogames have been my passion and it was always a way of working, I dreamt to follow. After a few years, out of the academic life, there was a need to have focus on it again, by choosing a career in videogames related to my childhood passion.

Meanwhile, I attended some events like Web Summit, got invited to represent the course and the University at Tech Days Aveiro 2019 and Moche XL 2019, participated in conferences and showcases like MILT Conference 2019, Lisbon Games Week 2019, Over & Out 2019 that guaranteed some recognition through some awards. None of this would be possible if I have not looked to improve myself, with the feedback given by people who crossed my path.

There is certainly still more to learn, competences to achieve and challenges to face that is why I am always paying attention to new opportunities in this domain and others within.
